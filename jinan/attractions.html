Daming Lake

Daming Lake is the largest lake in the city of Jinan, Shandong, China and one of city's main natural and cultural landmarks.


Thousand Buddha Mountain

Thousand Buddha Mountain is honored as the"city balcony", a "natural oxygen bar" where people can relax and enjoy entertainment.

Baotu Spring

Baotu Spring is known in China as the No 1 spring of the 72 in Jinan, "the spring city".
