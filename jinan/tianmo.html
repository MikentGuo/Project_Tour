Tianmo porridge

Tianmo is a type of salty porridge made of millet. The dish also features the use of ginger, pepper, spinach, peanut, thin sheets of bean curd and a dash of hot oil.

Can't wait to have a try? Make Tianmotang at Heping Road, Lixia District your first choice. As an inheritor of this intangible cultural heritage, Tianmotang, a time-honored restaurant in the city, feeds a multitude of mouths every morning.
