Why you should visit Xiamen?

SCENIC SPOTS

Gulangyu covers an area of 1.87 square kilometers, and has famous tourist attractions like Sunlight Rock, Shuzhuang Garden and Harmonium Museum. Here you may stroll to a quiet lane, or take a sunbath on the beach; there are Chinese and western architectures of various features.

DELICACIES

Stir fry the muscovy duck with sesame oil and then add mature ginger and rice wine to have it cooked. It smells appetizing and tastes sweet and spicy. It clears the liver and nourishes the lungs, tonifies the stomach and spleen, accelerate circulation of blood, and dispels the cold.

ROMANTIC TIMES

Completed and put into use in February 1987, Gulangyu Concert Hall is an important place for reception of high and serious music performances. Many art organizations from Japan, the US, Romania, Philippines, Singapore, Russia and Croatia, and symphony orchestras have give performances here. More importantly, concerts held at regular time, generally at 19:30, are free accessible to the public.

OVERVIEW OF XIAMEN

The coordinates of Xiamen are 118 ° 04 ′ 04 ″ E and 24 ° 26 ′ 46 ″ N. Xiamen is located in the southeast coast of China. It is made up of Xiamen Island, Gulangyu and some coastal areas on the north bank of inland Jiulong River. Altogether, these cover an area of 1,699.34 square kilometers, among them, Xiamen Island, the fourth largest island of Fujian covering an area of 141.09 square kilometers. It has a permanent population of 4,110,000 as of the end of 2018. It is divided into 6 districts- Siming, Huli, Jimei, Haicang, Tong'an, Xiang'an. It has awarded honors such as Four Consecutive National Civilized City, China's Outstanding Tourism City, National Model City for Environmental Protection, National Hygienic City, National Garden City, International Garden City and UN- Habitat Scroll of Honor Award.

Gulangyu covers an area of 1.87 square kilometers, and has famous tourist attractions like Sunlight Rock, Shuzhuang Garden and Harmonium Museum. Here you may stroll to a quiet lane, or take a sunbath on the beach; there are Chinese and western architectures of various features, and natural scenery.
